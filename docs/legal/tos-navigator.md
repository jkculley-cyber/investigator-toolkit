## 2. Description of Service

Navigator is a cloud-based ISS (In-School Suspension) and OSS (Out-of-School Suspension) referral and placement tracking module within the Waypoint platform. The Service includes:

- Referral intake and routing workflows for ISS/OSS placements
- Placement tracking with assigned staff and scheduling
- Support plan creation and intervention assignment
- Progress monitoring and placement completion workflows
- Reporting on referral patterns, placement outcomes, and support effectiveness
- Integration with Waypoint incident records and student profiles

Navigator shares the same cloud infrastructure, authentication system, and data architecture as the Waypoint platform.
