### 4. Pricing Strategy (Business Discussion — Not Implemented in Code)

Developed full 3-product pricing model. Key decisions:

**Model:** Enrollment-based bands (not per-campus) — scales cleanly across all ~1,200 Texas districts

| Band | Enrollment |
|------|-----------|
| Micro | ≤ 500 |
| Small | 501–2,500 |
| Medium | 2,501–10,000 |
| Large | 10,001–25,000 |
| Enterprise | 25,001+ (custom) |

**Starting-at prices (Micro band):**
| Product | Essential | Professional | Enterprise |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Waypoint | $2,500 | $4,500 | $7,000 |
| Navigator | $1,500 | $2,500 | $4,000 |
| Meridian | $2,000 | $3,500 | $5,500 |

**Bundle discounts:** 15% off any 2 products, 25% off all 3

**One-time fees:** Implementation $1,500–$3,500; Data migration $500–$2,000

**Multi-year:** 5% off 2-year, 10% off 3-year

**Products are sold standalone — no Waypoint prerequisite for Navigator or Meridian.**
