### 3. Compliance Whitepaper — `clearpath-site/whitepaper.html` (NEW FILE)

Created a complete 20-point DAEP compliance self-audit checklist as a print-optimized HTML document. Serves as a lead magnet for district outreach.

**Structure:**
- Fixed toolbar (screen only): "Save as PDF" button (`window.print()`) + "← Back to Site" link
- Cover page with org name, title, 2026 Edition badge
- Introduction — how to use, OCR audit risk context, legal disclaimer note on TEC citations
- **Section 1:** Placement Eligibility & Documentation (4 items)
- **Section 2:** SPED & 504 Manifestation Determination (5 items)
- **Section 3:** Transition Plan & Academic Continuity (5 items)
- **Section 4:** Parent Notification Requirements (3 items)
- **Section 5:** Re-Entry Planning (3 items)
- **Self-Audit Scorecard:** 20-item table with print-ready empty checkboxes
- **Scoring bands:** 18–20 Compliant | 14–17 At Risk | <14 Urgent Review
- **Back page:** Waypoint/Navigator/Meridian product callout cards + "Schedule a Free Demo →" CTA
- **Legal disclaimer** — citations for reference only, not legal advice

TEC citations are displayed in amber `⚠ Verify TEC Citation` callout boxes, explicitly labeled as needing legal verification.

**Print CSS:** `@page { size: letter; margin: 0.75in }`. Toolbar hidden on print. `-webkit-print-color-adjust: exact` applied to colored elements.
